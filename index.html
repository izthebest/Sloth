About

Sloth is a social connectivity platform designed to allow teens the opportunity to release all their fears and troubles to a new friend. Peers who are honest and care will listen and help others find a way to navigate their mental illness, such as anxiety, or social dramas while relaxing with a cup of Starbucks.

You begin by signing up for a Sloth account where you will be asked a few questions about yourself. Then we will connect you with some people who share similar traits in the nearby area. From there you can chat or meet up in person at the closest Starbucks Coffee Shop where you can recieve 10% off your purchase when you use the Sloth code.

The Sloth mobile website was designed to help teens a support system of strangers who have or are currently struggling with some of the same issues their peers are facing.

BEHIND THE IDEA

Four teens attending the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in Seattle came up with Sloth as their final project to help peers who might be suffering from bullying or depression.

ABOUT

LOCATION

We respect your privacy which is why we don't save or ask for your location. Instead you choose which nearby Starbucks coffee shops you would possibly like to meet someone.
